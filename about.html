About Marks Commercial Services

Family-owned commercial oven and kitchen equipment repair serving restaurants, schools, hospitals, churches, and commercial kitchens across Central Arkansas.

Mark Ball

Owner & Lead Technician

With over 30 years of experience in commercial kitchen equipment service, I take pride in providing honest, high-quality repair and maintenance for local businesses across Arkansas.

Our Commitment to Your Kitchen

Our mission is simple: to provide quality commercial kitchen equipment repair with a personal touch. As a “Mom & Pop” style service company, we believe in treating our customers like partners, not just transactions. We understand how critical your ovens, fryers, and hot equipment are to your business, and we work hard to keep your kitchen running smoothly.

We focus on fair pricing, clear communication, and workmanship you can trust. Unlike large corporate service providers, we take the time to listen, explain your options, and stand behind every repair we make. For us, it’s not about quick profits—it’s about earning long-term trust and being the first call you make when your commercial kitchen equipment needs attention.

About Mark

I began my career in service work in 1989, working alongside my father in his McDonald’s franchise. From there, I went on to work with Bromley Parts and Service, gaining over 28 years of hands-on experience in the commercial foodservice industry.

Over the years, I’ve earned my CFESA Master Technician certification and have had the opportunity to work in countless kitchens across Central Arkansas—and throughout the state. That experience has taught me more than just how to repair equipment. It’s shown me the value of building relationships, being reliable, and doing every job with fairness, honesty, and professionalism.

Today, through Marks Commercial Services, I’m proud to bring that same level of care to every commercial oven, fryer, grill, and piece of kitchen equipment we service.
